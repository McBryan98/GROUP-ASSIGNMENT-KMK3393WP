    Modified of Banana Stem as an Oil Absorbent

Final Year Project for Diploma in Petrochemical (Process) 2017 Polytechnic Kuching, Sarawak.. It is a project of modified a banana stem as an oil absorbent. The process is quiet hard to explain but it mix of Stearic Acid and Calcium Carbonate. Then both mixture mix with the banana stem. Then after a few days to keep it dry, we try it on a few types of oil which is cooking oil and engine oil. Then the result is quiet supprising us it successfully absorb the oil.
