The Business Concept and Background

The business that I will do is about a food delivery services to the town of Kapit. Due to the recent pandemic, not many folks get to gather around town for some meals as the government enforces strict law against those who are in lockdown. So, if there is a food delivery services are available for the town, (since there is no Grab Food/Food Panda services available in Kapit) the townsfolk can enjoy their meal at their own home.

Strategy & Planning

In this section, I will explain the needed strategy and planning needed for this to work
Social Media

For starter, a promotional campaign will be need across social media to attract customer which is main source used in this advertisement. A group of 5 people in which consists of 2 delivery man, 1 investor/manager and 2 cooks for the meals. Main communication method used by the customer to order is through Whatsapp or Facebook.

Kapit's Wet Market

Recipes for meals stride for variety and dependent on the local produces as the supplies for the cooking will be home bought/made. For this reason, most of the supplies will be acquired in the wet market and supermarket that are available.

Activities Involved

This section will explain a little bit about the 4 main activities involved in order for the business to run.

Preparation of the Foods

For the most part, all the cooking will done using modern gas stove with 2 chefs handling the kitchens. All the appliances will be prepared by the manager with a little investment from him. However, some meals like the pansuh will be needed to be prepared in a traditional method like in a fireplace and a bamboo.

"So when people ask me what do you think of Michelin? I don't cook for guide, I cook for customers."

Delivery Services

The delivery services are done using motorcycles which belong to the 2 delivery man. Depending on the weathers and the amount of customers, car will be also be used in the delivery of the customer's meals.

Management

Management is the key for the successfulness of a business. The manager will be responsible for the state of the business such as logistics, financial state, profit and involve in negotiating a price for the supplies.

Investment

Investment are done by the families of the member of the group to support their business. The manager who is also the main financer of this business will try to get a sponsor if possible once the business have made profit
